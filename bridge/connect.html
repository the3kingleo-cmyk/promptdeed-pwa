Gemini ↔ Shopify Bridge

Connect the two sides, then test that they actually talk to each other. Your keys stay on this device only — they are never uploaded anywhere.

1 Connect Google Gemini not connected

Tap the button to open Google's page. You log in with your own Google account and create a key, then paste it back here.

Open Google login & get my key →

2 Connect Shopify not connected

From your Shopify admin: Settings → Apps → Develop apps → Create an app → Admin API, allow read_products, install, and copy the token.

3 Run the bridge

Once Gemini is connected, type any product name and Gemini will write store copy for it — proving the bridge works end to end.


  


  🔒 Everything you type here is saved only in this browser (localStorage) and sent only to Google and Shopify directly. Nothing is sent to us, and nothing is committed to GitHub.
